La Niña de la Uva

Full Writing Sample by Amairani Ceballos

Full Sample

I tried to carry the ladder, but I was quickly humbled by its weight as it toppled onto my head. My father chuckled as he watched me cover up my urge to cry with frustration...

His office was the many fields of orange trees, kissed by the fresh morning air. He worked through the day, trying to fill as many harvest bins as possible...

It was hard. More difficult than I gave that old man credit for. A whole career of manual labor. The same routine. I was ready for retirement upon the first two days of working...

At home, I lay on the warm carpet, tangled in front of the large TV. I reflected on life lessons, gratitude, and experiences from my childhood through college...

There were many chilling auras near me watching every person who arrived. While waiting for an Uber ride on the Tijuana side, I saw a guy my age walking alongside his father...

I was intent on making the most out of my summer harvesting grapes and tasting the rural lifestyle of a traditional Mexican mother working in the fields of the Central Valley.
